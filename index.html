How to Play

  1. Click on Start Game
  2. Select a Level
  3. There will be a number of sections created for the selected level
  4. Click on 1 of the sections to take a shot at goal
  5. After each kick, click on Next Kick to continue with the game.
  6. You get 10 chances to score as many goals as possible

Click on End Game, to restart from the beginning
